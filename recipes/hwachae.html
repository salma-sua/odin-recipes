Hwachae

Description

Hwachae, or specifically subak-hwachae, is also known as Korean watermelon punch. It's a refreshing summer fruit punch made with watermelon, fruit cocktail, lemon-lime soda, and milk. It's super easy to make and is children and family-friendly, which makes it great for get-togethers!

Ingredients

  • Seedless Watermelon
  • Milk
  • Lemon-lime soda
  • Sweet condensed milk, honey or sugar
  • Ice cubes: optional

Steps

  1. Mix milk and sweet condensed milk. You can use honey or sugar instead (Adjust the amount according to your preference).
  2. Add the soda and mix well.
  3. Combine with the watermelon (and other fruits if using) in a large mixing bowl.
